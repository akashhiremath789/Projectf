Aloo Gobi Masala

Aloo Gobi Masala

Ingredients

  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 small cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder and tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds; let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Mix in ginger-garlic paste and fry until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook till tomatoes soften.
  5. Add potatoes and cauliflower; stir to coat well with the masala.
  6. Cover and cook on low flame for 10–12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Once vegetables are tender, sprinkle garam masala and mix gently.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with roti or rice.
