Uttar Karnataka Rotti

Ingredients

  • 2 cups jowar (sorghum) flour
  • Warm water, as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or oil for cooking (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix jowar flour and salt.
  2. Gradually add warm water and knead into a soft, pliable dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls.
  4. Take one ball and flatten it gently with your hands into a round rotti (no rolling pin needed).
  5. Heat a tava (griddle) on medium heat and cook the rotti, flipping occasionally, until both sides are cooked and lightly browned.
  6. Optionally, brush with ghee or oil while cooking for extra flavor.
  7. Serve hot with curries, chutneys, or vegetables as part of a traditional North Karnataka meal.
