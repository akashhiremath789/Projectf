Chips & Crisps

Ingredients

  • Potatoes – 4 to 5 (medium-sized)
  • Salt – as per taste
  • Oil – for deep frying
  • Optional: Chili powder, pepper, or other seasonings

Steps

    1. Wash and Peel

  1. Wash the potatoes thoroughly.
  2. Peel the skin using a peeler. (You can also keep the skin for rustic-style chips.)

    3. 2. Slice Thinly

  3. Slice the potatoes into thin, even rounds using a slicer or knife.
  4. Thinner slices give crispier chips.

    5. 3. Soak in Water

  5. Place the slices in a bowl of cold water for 15–20 minutes.
  6. This removes excess starch and prevents them from sticking.

    7. 4. Dry the Slices

  7. Drain the water and pat the slices dry with a clean towel or tissue paper.
  8. Make sure there’s no moisture — this helps them fry evenly.

    9. 5. Heat the Oil

  9. Pour enough oil into a deep frying pan.
  10. Heat it on medium flame (around 170–180°C).

    11. 6. Fry the Chips

  11. Add a few potato slices at a time (don’t overcrowd).
  12. Fry until golden and crisp, stirring occasionally.
  13. Remove them once the bubbling reduces.

    14. 7. Drain Excess Oil

  14. Place the fried chips on paper towels to absorb extra oil.

    15. 8. Season

  15. Sprinkle salt and other seasonings (like chili powder, pepper, or masala) while still warm.

    16. 9. Cool & Store

  16. Let them cool completely before storing in an airtight container to keep them crispy.
