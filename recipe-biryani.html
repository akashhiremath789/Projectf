Chicken Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 1 cup curd
  • 2 onions (fried)
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • Mint, coriander, ghee

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with curd and spices for 1 hour.
  2. Cook basmati rice till 70% done.
  3. Layer rice, chicken, fried onions, and herbs.
  4. Seal pot and cook on dum for 20 mins.
  5. Garnish and serve hot!
