Malabar Chicken Biryani

Paneer Kadai

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup mixed bell peppers (capsicum), chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions till translucent.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté till aromatic.
  3. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder; cook till soft.
  4. Add bell peppers and cook for 3–4 minutes until slightly tender.
  5. Add paneer cubes and sprinkle garam masala. Mix gently and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Adjust salt and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
