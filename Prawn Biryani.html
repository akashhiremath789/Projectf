Prawn Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g prawns (cleaned and deveined)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions (thinly sliced and fried)
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Mint and coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Salt and chili powder to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate prawns with yogurt, biryani masala, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and chili for 30–60 minutes.
  2. Cook basmati rice until 70% done and drain excess water.
  3. In a heavy-bottomed pot, layer rice, marinated prawns, fried onions, and fresh herbs.
  4. Drizzle ghee on top, seal the pot, and cook on dum (low heat) for 20 minutes.
  5. Gently mix the layers and serve hot with raita or salad.
