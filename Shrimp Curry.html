Shrimp Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh shrimp (cleaned and deveined)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp curry powder or masala
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt and chili powder to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  3. Stir in tomato puree and cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add spices and mix well to form a rich masala base.
  5. Add shrimp and cook for 3–4 minutes until they turn pink.
  6. Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 5–7 minutes on low flame.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with steamed rice or roti.
