Idli-sambar

Ingredients

  • 2 cups idli rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • ½ tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt, to taste
  • Water, as needed
  • Oil for greasing idli molds
  • For sambar: 1 cup toor dal, 2-3 tomatoes, 1 onion, tamarind paste, sambar powder, curry leaves, salt, and spices

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours. Add fenugreek seeds to dal.
  2. Grind dal and rice to a smooth batter and mix together. Add salt and ferment overnight.
  3. Grease idli molds and pour batter. Steam for 10-12 minutes until idlis are cooked.
  4. Cook toor dal for sambar until soft. Blend tomatoes and cook with dal, tamarind, sambar powder, and spices. Add curry leaves.
  5. Serve hot idlis with sambar and coconut chutney.
