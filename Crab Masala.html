Crab Masala

Ingredients

  • 500g fresh crab (cleaned and cut into pieces)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp crab masala or curry powder
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt and red chili powder to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  3. Stir in tomato puree and cook until oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add crab masala, chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add crab pieces and cook for 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Pour in coconut milk, cover, and simmer for 10–12 minutes until the crab is fully cooked.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with steamed rice or roti.
