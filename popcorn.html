popcorn

Ingredients

  • Popcorn kernels - ½ cup
  • Oil or butter - 2 to 3 tbsp
  • Salt - as per taste
  • Optional: Cheese powder, chili flakes, caramel, or other seasonings

Steps

    1. Heat the Oil

  1. Pour oil or butter into a deep, heavy-bottomed pan.
  2. Heat it on medium flame until slightly hot (not smoking).

    3. 2. Test the Temperature

  3. Add 2-3 kernels to the oil and cover the pan.
  4. Once they pop, the oil is ready for the rest of the kernels.

    5. 3. Add Kernels

  5. Add the remaining popcorn kernels and cover the pan with a lid.
  6. Shake the pan gently to spread the heat evenly.

    7. 4. Pop the Corn

  7. Keep the pan covered while the kernels pop.
  8. Reduce the flame slightly once popping becomes frequent.
  9. When popping slows down, turn off the heat.

    10. 5. Season

  10. Transfer the popcorn to a large bowl.
  11. Sprinkle salt and your favorite seasonings while still warm.

    12. 6. Serve or Store

  12. Enjoy immediately for best crunch, or store in an airtight container once cooled.
