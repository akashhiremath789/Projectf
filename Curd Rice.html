Curd Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice (preferably cooled)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt / curd
  • 1/4 cup milk (optional, for creaminess)
  • 1–2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • For tempering: 1 tsp mustard seeds, 1–2 dried red chilies, 8–10 curry leaves, 1 tsp urad dal, 1 tsp oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Cook rice and let it cool to room temperature.
  2. Mix yogurt (and milk if using) with the cooled rice until creamy and smooth.
  3. Add chopped green chilies, grated ginger, coriander leaves, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Prepare tempering: heat oil/ghee, add mustard seeds, dried red chilies, curry leaves, and urad dal. Fry until aromatic.
  5. Pour the tempering over the curd rice and mix gently.
  6. Serve chilled or at room temperature as a comforting South Indian meal.
