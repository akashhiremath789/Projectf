Pizza Margherita

Ingredients

  • Pizza base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Spread tomato sauce over pizza base.
  2. Add mozzarella slices and basil leaves.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Bake at 220°C for 10–12 minutes.
  5. Slice and enjoy hot!
