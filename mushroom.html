Mushroom Biryani

Ingredients

  • 400g mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • Mint and coriander leaves for garnish
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate mushrooms with curd, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and salt for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Cook basmati rice with a little salt and drain when 90% done.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan, fry onions till golden brown, and set aside some for garnishing.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and chopped tomatoes to the remaining onions; sauté till soft.
  5. Mix in marinated mushrooms and cook till tender and aromatic.
  6. In a large pot, layer cooked rice and mushroom masala alternately.
  7. Top with fried onions, mint, and coriander leaves. Drizzle a little ghee.
  8. Cover and steam (dum) on low flame for 10–12 minutes.
  9. Fluff gently and serve hot with raita or pickle.
