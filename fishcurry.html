butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g fish pieces (pomfret, seer fish, or kingfish)
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 8–10 curry leaves
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 cup thick coconut milk
  • 1 cup thin coconut milk or water
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Clean the fish pieces, wash gently, and set aside.
  2. Heat coconut oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, and let them splutter.
  3. Add curry leaves and sauté onions until golden brown.
  4. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and pulpy.
  6. Mix in turmeric, chili powder, and coriander powder; sauté for a minute.
  7. Pour in thin coconut milk (or water), add green chilies, and bring to a gentle boil.
  8. Add the fish pieces carefully and cook on low heat for 8–10 minutes until tender.
  9. Pour in thick coconut milk and simmer for another 2–3 minutes (do not boil after this step).
  10. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with steamed rice or appam.
