butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 300g boneless chicken(preferably cooled)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ cup cream, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • For tempering: 1 tsp mustard seeds, ½ cup cream

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt for 30 minutes.
  2. Grill or pan-fry until half cooked.
  3. In another pan, heat butter and add tomato puree and spices.
  4. Add chicken and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Stir in cream and simmer mix gently.
  6. Serve with naan or jeera rice
