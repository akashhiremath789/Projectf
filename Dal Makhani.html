Dal Makhani

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole black urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 cup rajma (kidney beans)
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp butter or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp chili powder, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste, fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Soak urad dal and rajma overnight or at least 6–8 hours.
  2. Cook soaked dal and rajma in water until soft (pressure cooker or pot).
  3. In a pan, heat butter/ghee, add cumin seeds, chopped onions, and sauté until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomato puree, chili powder, turmeric, and cook till oil separates.
  5. Add cooked dal and rajma, simmer for 15–20 minutes on low heat.
  6. Stir in cream, adjust salt, and sprinkle garam masala. Simmer for 5 more minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.
