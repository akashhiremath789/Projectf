butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton, cut into medium pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely sliced
  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and sauté onions until they turn golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Mix in the mutton pieces and cook for 5–7 minutes until slightly browned.
  4. Whisk yogurt with chili, coriander, and turmeric powder, then add to the pan.
  5. Cook on medium flame until oil separates and mutton is well coated in the masala.
  6. Add 1½ cups of water, cover, and simmer until the meat becomes tender.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala, mix well, and cook for another 2 minutes.
  8. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with steamed rice or naan.
