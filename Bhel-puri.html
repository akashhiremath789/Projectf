Bhel-puri

Ingredients

  • Puffed rice (murmura) - 2 cups
  • Sev - ½ cup
  • Boiled potatoes - 1 (chopped)
  • Onion - 1 (finely chopped)
  • Tomato - 1 (finely chopped)
  • Green chutney - 2 tbsp
  • Tamarind chutney - 2 tbsp
  • Roasted peanuts - 2 tbsp (optional)
  • Coriander leaves - for garnish
  • Lemon juice - 1 tsp
  • Salt and chaat masala - as per taste

Steps

    1. Prepare the Ingredients

  1. Finely chop onion, tomato, and boiled potato.
  2. Keep green chutney, tamarind chutney, and sev ready.

    3. 2. Mix the Base

  3. In a large mixing bowl, add puffed rice, chopped onions, tomatoes, and potatoes.
  4. Mix them gently to combine.

    5. 3. Add Flavor

  5. Add green chutney and tamarind chutney as per your taste.
  6. Sprinkle salt, chaat masala, and a dash of lemon juice.
  7. Mix everything quickly so the puffed rice remains crisp.

    8. 4. Garnish

  8. Top with sev, roasted peanuts, and chopped coriander leaves.

    9. 5. Serve

  9. Serve immediately in bowls or paper cones to enjoy the crunch and flavor.
