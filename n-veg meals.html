Non-Veg Full Meal

Ingredients

  • 2 cups steamed rice
  • 1 cup chicken curry (spicy or mild as preferred)
  • 1 piece fried fish or chicken fry
  • 1 bowl sambar or rasam
  • 1 small bowl curd
  • 1 serving vegetable poriyal or sabzi
  • 1 papad and pickle
  • 1 small sweet or dessert

Steps

  1. Cook rice and keep it warm.
  2. Prepare chicken curry using your favorite masala blend.
  3. Fry fish or chicken until golden and crispy.
  4. Cook sambar or rasam to add a tangy flavor to the meal.
  5. Prepare a simple vegetable side dish and fry papad.
  6. Arrange all items neatly on a plate or banana leaf.
  7. Serve hot with curd, pickle, and a small sweet to finish!
