Masala Dosa

Ingredients

    1)For Dosa Batter:

  • 2 cups rice (regular or parboiled rice)
  • ½ cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • tsp fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • Water (as needed)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Oil or ghee (for cooking dosa)

    • 2) For Potato Masala (Filling):

  • 3–4 medium potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp split urad dal
  • A few curry leaves
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Heat a non-stick tawa or iron dosa pan.
  2. Pour a ladleful of batter in the center and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa
  3. Drizzle a little oil or ghee around the edges.
  4. Cook on medium heat until the dosa turns golden brown and crispy
  5. Place 2–3 tbsp of potato masala in the center.
  6. Fold the dosa from both sides.
