Malabar Chicken Biryani

Hakka Noodles

Ingredients

  • 200g Hakka noodles
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 capsicum (bell pepper), thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Spring onion for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil noodles as per packet instructions, drain, and toss with a little oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Heat oil in a large wok or pan over high flame.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 30 seconds.
  4. Add onion, carrot, capsicum, and cabbage. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes on high flame.
  5. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, tomato ketchup, pepper, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the cooked noodles and toss everything together until evenly coated with sauce.
  7. Garnish with chopped spring onion and serve hot.
