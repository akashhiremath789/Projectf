Bhajji

Ingredients

  • Gram flour (besan) - 1 cup
  • Vegetables (onion, potato, chili, brinjal, etc.) - sliced
  • Rice flour - 2 tbsp (for extra crispiness)
  • Red chili powder - 1 tsp
  • Turmeric powder - ¼ tsp
  • Salt - as per taste
  • Water - as needed to make batter
  • Oil - for deep frying

Steps

    1. Prepare the Vegetables

  1. Wash and slice the vegetables thinly and evenly.
  2. Keep them aside on a dry plate.

    3. 2. Make the Batter

  3. In a mixing bowl, add gram flour, rice flour, chili powder, turmeric, and salt.
  4. Gradually add water to make a smooth, thick batter (not too runny).

    5. 3. Heat the Oil

  5. Pour oil into a deep frying pan.
  6. Heat it on medium flame until it’s hot enough for frying.

    7. 4. Dip and Fry

  7. Dip each vegetable slice into the batter, coating it evenly.
  8. Gently drop into the hot oil and fry until golden and crisp.
  9. Flip occasionally for even frying.

    10. 5. Drain Excess Oil

  10. Remove the bhajjis and place them on paper towels to absorb excess oil.

    11. 6. Serve

  11. Serve hot with chutney or tomato ketchup for a perfect tea-time snack.
