Upma

Ingredients

  • 1 cup semolina (rava/sooji)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 cups water
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Optional: curry leaves, chopped vegetables (carrot, peas)

Steps

  1. Dry roast semolina in a pan until lightly golden. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds. Once they splutter, add green chilies, curry leaves, and onions.
  3. Add water and salt, bring it to a boil.
  4. Gradually add roasted semolina, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  5. Cook on low heat until water is absorbed and upma reaches desired consistency.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
