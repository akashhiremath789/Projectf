Coconut Rice

Coconut Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cooled)
  • 1 cup freshly grated coconut
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1–2 dried red chilies
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp cashews or peanuts
  • 1 tsp urad dal and 1 tsp chana dal
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat coconut oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter.
  2. Add dried red chilies, curry leaves, urad dal, chana dal, and cashews. Fry until golden.
  3. Stir in the grated coconut and sauté lightly for 1–2 minutes without browning.
  4. Add cooked rice and salt, then mix gently to coat the rice evenly with the coconut mixture.
  5. Cook for 2–3 minutes on low flame to let flavors blend together.
  6. Serve warm with papad, pickle, or vegetable kurma for a simple South Indian dinner.
