Creme Caramel

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar (for caramel)
  • 2 tablespoons water (for caramel)
  • 2 cups full-fat milk
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup sugar (for custard)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Prepare caramel: In a saucepan, combine 1/2 cup sugar and 2 tablespoons water. Heat on medium flame without stirring until the sugar melts and turns golden brown. Quickly pour the caramel into ramekins or a baking dish, swirling to coat the bottom evenly. Set aside to cool and harden.
  2. Prepare custard: In another saucepan, heat milk until just warm (do not boil). In a separate bowl, whisk eggs, 1/2 cup sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt until smooth. Gradually add the warm milk while whisking continuously to prevent curdling.
  3. Assemble: Strain the custard mixture through a fine sieve and pour it gently over the hardened caramel in the ramekins.
  4. Bake: Place the ramekins in a baking dish filled halfway with hot water (water bath). Bake in a preheated oven at 160°C (320°F) for 40–45 minutes, or until the custard is set but still slightly jiggly in the center.
  5. Cool and chill: Remove from the oven and let cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  6. Unmold and serve: To serve, run a knife around the edges of the ramekin, place a plate on top, and invert carefully. The caramel will flow over the custard beautifully.
  7. Serve chilled and enjoy the silky, creamy Crème Caramel dessert!
