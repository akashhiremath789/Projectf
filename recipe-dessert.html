Chocolate Dessert

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup melted chocolate

Steps

  1. Mix flour, cocoa, sugar, and baking powder.
  2. Add milk and melted chocolate. Stir well.
  3. Pour into baking pan and bake for 25 minutes.
  4. Cool, frost with chocolate cream, and serve.
