Mixture

Ingredients

  • Sev (omapodi) - 1 cup
  • Boondi - ½ cup
  • Flattened rice (poha) - ½ cup
  • Roasted peanuts - ¼ cup
  • Cashew nuts - 2 tbsp
  • Curry leaves - a few
  • Red chili powder - ½ tsp
  • Turmeric powder - ¼ tsp
  • Salt - as per taste
  • Oil - for deep frying

Steps

    1. Fry the Ingredients

  1. Heat oil in a deep frying pan on medium flame.
  2. Fry the poha until crisp and light; remove and keep aside.
  3. Fry peanuts, cashews, and curry leaves until golden brown; set aside.

    4. 2. Add Sev and Boondi

  4. Fry sev and boondi lightly if not already fried.
  5. Place all fried items in a large mixing bowl.

    6. 3. Mix the Spices

  6. Sprinkle red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt over the mixture.
  7. Toss everything gently to coat evenly while still slightly warm.

    8. 4. Cool and Combine

  8. Let the mixture cool completely before storing.
  9. Mix all ingredients well so the flavors blend together.

    10. 5. Store

  10. Store in an airtight container to keep it fresh and crunchy for weeks.

    11. 6. Serve

  11. Serve as a crispy, spicy tea-time snack or a festive treat.
