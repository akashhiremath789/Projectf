Chicken Korma

Ingredients

  • 400g boneless chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 1 large onion, finely sliced
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, and salt for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan and sauté sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Mix in the marinated chicken and cook for 8–10 minutes until it starts to soften.
  5. Sprinkle garam masala and add a little water if needed for gravy.
  6. Pour in cream and simmer for 5 minutes until the curry becomes thick and flavorful.
  7. Garnish with a few fried onions and serve hot with naan or jeera rice.
