Mutter Paneer

Ingredients

  • 200 g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 cup cream (optional)
  • Salt to taste, fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil/ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds and sauté until aromatic.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1–2 minutes.
  4. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add green peas and paneer cubes, mix gently, and cook for 5–7 minutes.
  6. Stir in cream (if using) and garam masala, simmer for 2–3 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with roti, naan, or rice.
