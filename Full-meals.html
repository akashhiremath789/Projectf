Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 5500 g mixed vegetables + paneer
  • 1 cup curd
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced & fried until golden
  • 2 tbsp garam masala / thali masala
  • Mint, coriander, ghee
  • MGinger-garlic paste, salt, chili, turmeric, and other spices

Steps

  1. Marinate vegetables/paneer with yogurt, ginger-garlic, and spices for 30–60 minutes.
  2. CPartially cook the basmati rice (about 70% done).
  3. In a heavy pot or handi, layer rice, marinated mix, fried onions, mint, coriander, and drizzle ghee.
  4. Seal the pot (with dough or lid) and cook on dum (low flame) for 20–25 minutes until done.
  5. Open, fluff gently, garnish with fresh herbs and serve hot with raita or salad.
