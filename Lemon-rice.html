Lemon-rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cooled)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 dried red chili (optional)
  • 1 tbsp urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1 tbsp chana dal (split Bengal gram)
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • ¼ tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice (adjust to taste)
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts or cashews
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add urad dal, chana dal, red chili, and curry leaves. Sauté until the dals turn golden brown.
  3. Add chopped green chilies and turmeric powder. Mix well.
  4. Add cooked rice and salt. Toss gently to combine all ingredients evenly.
  5. Turn off the heat and add lemon juice. Mix well without mashing the rice.
  6. Garnish with roasted peanuts and fresh coriander leaves.
  7. Serve warm with papad or pickle.
