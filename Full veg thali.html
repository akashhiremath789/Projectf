Full veg thali

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 2 chapatis / rotis
  • 1 serving dal (like Dal Tadka or Dal Makhani)
  • 1–2 vegetable curries (like Aloo Gobi, Bhindi Masala)
  • 1 serving raita or curd
  • 1 small portion of salad (cucumber, tomato, carrot)
  • 1 small sweet (like Gulab Jamun or Kheer)
  • 1–2 tsp pickle or chutney
  • Ghee or butter for serving

Steps

  1. Cook rice and chapatis/rotis separately and keep warm.
  2. Prepare dal and vegetable curries as per recipes.
  3. Make raita or curd side dish and fresh salad.
  4. Arrange rice, chapati, dal, vegetable curries, raita, salad, sweet, and pickle on a plate.
  5. Drizzle a little ghee or butter over the rice and serve the thali hot.
  6. Enjoy the wholesome and balanced flavors of a traditional Indian full veg meal.
